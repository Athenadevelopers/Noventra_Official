What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 3-6 months. I provide detailed timelines during the planning phase and always deliver on schedule.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer various support packages including 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, security patches, and technical support to ensure your solution runs smoothly and stays up-to-date.

What technologies do you use?

I use modern, industry-standard technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, PHP, Python, and various databases. I choose the best technology stack for each project based on requirements and scalability needs.

Can you work with existing systems?

Absolutely! I can integrate with your existing systems, APIs, and databases. I'll analyze your current setup and recommend the best integration approach to ensure seamless operation.

Do you provide hosting services?

Yes, I offer hosting solutions with reliable cloud infrastructure, regular backups, SSL certificates, and performance optimization to ensure your application runs smoothly and securely.

I maintain regular communication through email, phone calls, and video meetings. I provide weekly progress updates and am always available for urgent matters. Clear communication is key to project success.
