The Story Behind Noventra

Founded by Sahan Weerasinghe, Noventra Web Solutions was born from a passion for bridging the gap between sophisticated technology and tangible business growth. Based in Sri Lanka, we specialize in architecting high-performance full-stack ecosystems and native mobile experiences. With a foundation built on technical precision and creative problem-solving, Noventra is dedicated to transforming ambitious ideas into scalable digital realities.

My journey began with a vision to make cutting-edge technology a catalyst for growth. Today, that vision lives through Noventra Web Solutions (Pvt) Ltd. We specialize in building the digital backbone of modern businesses—leveraging PHP/MySQL for scalable web applications and Kotlin with Jetpack Compose for next-generation Android development. Having successfully delivered projects for startups and enterprises worldwide, we continue to transform ambitious ideas into high-performance digital realities.
